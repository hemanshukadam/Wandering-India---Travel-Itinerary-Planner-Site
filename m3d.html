3 Day Mumbai Luxury Itinerary

Day 1

Morning: Begin your day with a sumptuous breakfast at the iconic Britannia & Co. Restaurant, known for its Parsi delicacies. Afterward, immerse yourself in the rich history of Mumbai with a private guided tour of the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya (CSMVS), a treasure trove of art and artifacts.

Afternoon: Indulge in a luxurious seafood lunch at Gajalee, renowned for its coastal cuisine. Post that, take a leisurely walk through the charming lanes of Kala Ghoda, home to numerous art galleries and designer boutiques.

Evening: Enjoy a sophisticated dinner at The Bombay Canteen, a modern Indian restaurant. Conclude your evening with a private night sightseeing tour of Mumbai's iconic landmarks, including the Gateway of India and the Marine Drive.

Day 2

Morning: Start your day with a lavish breakfast at Indigo Delicatessen, a popular all-day dining spot. Afterward, experience the grandeur of Mumbai with a private tour of the Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Afternoon: Enjoy a fine dining experience at Wasabi by Morimoto - The Taj Mahal Palace Hotel, known for its exquisite Japanese cuisine. Following lunch, explore the upscale boutiques and designer stores at Colaba Causeway.

Evening: Dine at the award-winning Trishna, famous for its seafood specialties. Conclude your day with a visit to the vibrant Bandra-Worli Sea Link (Rajiv Gandhi Sea Link), especially beautiful at night.

Day 3

Morning: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at The Table, a contemporary restaurant with a focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients. After breakfast, take a private tour of the bustling Crawford Market and the nearby Mangaldas Market for a shopping experience.

Afternoon: Savor a traditional North Indian meal at Peshawri-ITC Maratha, Mumbai, known for its authentic flavors. Post lunch, unwind with a relaxing spa session at the Jiva Spa in the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel.

Evening: Conclude your luxurious Mumbai experience with a farewell dinner at Ziya - The Oberoi, a fine dining restaurant offering a contemporary take on Indian cuisine. After dinner, take a leisurely walk along the serene Marine Drive, enjoying the sea breeze.

Budget

Estimated budget: ₹70,000 - ₹80,000
