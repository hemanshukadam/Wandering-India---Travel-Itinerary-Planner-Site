3-Day Mumbai Itinerary

Day 1: Mumbai Classic Experience

Morning: Start your day with a classic Mumbai breakfast at Kyani & Co. Then, visit the iconic Gateway of India and take a short walk to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT).

Afternoon: Have lunch at Sardar Pav Bhaji and explore Crawford Market.

Evening: Enjoy a leisurely walk along Marine Drive and indulge in street food at Chowpatty Beach. End the day with a drink at Leopold Cafe.

Day 2: Cultural and Historical Exploration

Morning: Have a South Indian breakfast at Copper Chimney and take a guided tour of Dharavi Slum.

Afternoon: Enjoy a thali lunch at Shree Thaker Bhojanalay and visit Mani Bhavan Gandhi Sangrahalaya.

Evening: Experience a street food tour and have dinner at Bademiya.

Day 3: Heritage Walk and Modern Experience

Morning: Join a South Mumbai Heritage Walking Tour and explore Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya.

Afternoon: Enjoy lunch at Britannia & Co. Restaurant and visit Elephanta Caves.

Evening: Have dinner at The Bombay Canteen and take a stroll along Bandra-Worli Sea Link.

Budget

The estimated budget for this itinerary is between ₹5000 and ₹6000.
